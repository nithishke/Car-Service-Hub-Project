The Tata Harrier is a mid-size SUV known for its bold exterior design and robust build quality. Launched in 2019, it features Tata's Impact Design 2.0 philosophy, giving
it a distinctive presence on the road. Powered by a 2.0-liter Kryotec diesel engine, it offers a balance of performance and fuel efficiency. The interior is spacious
and well-appointed, with premium materials and modern amenities including a touchscreen infotainment system. Safety features include multiple airbags, ABS with EBD,
traction control, and hill hold assist. It provides a comfortable ride quality suitable for both city driving and long highway journeys. The Harrier's suspension
setup ensures stability and comfort over varying road conditions. Overall, it's a compelling choice for buyers seeking a stylish, feature-packed SUV with strong
performance and safety credentials.
