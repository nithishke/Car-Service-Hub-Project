The car was introduced on October 4, 2021. It is a subcompact crossover with front-wheel drive. It is equipped with a 1.2-liter three-cylinder Revotron gasoline engine,
which is also used in some other models of this brand.It has two additional modifications: Kaziranga — in Grassland Beige colour with leatherette inserts in the interior
and rhino mascot on the side of the body; Camo — in Foliage Green colour and super-durable seats with a camouflage print, as well as the Camo symbol on the body and
two roof options (white and black).

● Kaziranga — in Grassland Beige colour with leatherette inserts in the interior and rhino mascot on the side of the body;

● Camo — in Foliage Green colour and super-durable seats with a camouflage print, as well as the Camo symbol on the body and two roof options (white and black).

The first model was released in blue and also has a textile interior with a unique Tri-Arrow upholstery design. The Kaziranga begins production in February 2022
and the Camo 7 months later in September 2022.
